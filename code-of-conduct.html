code of conduct

We believe only in White Hat Hacking. On the other hand, we condemn Black Hat Hacking.


The articles, tutorials, and demos provided by TCA. are for informational and educational purposes only, and for those who’re willing and curious to know and learn about Ethical Hacking, Security, and Penetration Testing.


Any time the word “Hacking” that is used on this site shall be regarded as Ethical Hacking.


Do not attempt to violate the law with anything contained here. If you planned to use the content for illegal purposes, then please leave this Club immediately! We will not be responsible for any illegal actions. Neither administration of the University, the authors of the materials, or anyone else affiliated in any way, is going to accept responsibility for your actions.


The misuse of the information in this Club can result in criminal charges brought against the persons in question. The authors, TCA or RSU will not be held responsible in the event any criminal charges be brought against any individuals misusing the information in this website to break the law.


You shall not misuse the information to gain unauthorized access. However, you may try out these hacks on your computer at your own risk. Performing hack attempts (without permission) on computers that you do not own is illegal.



The Cyber Alliance Club has a zero-tolerance policy for inappropriate behaviour.


If you experience or witness any of the following behaviour at a TCA. event, or on the TCA websites or social media, please report it immediately.


  • Racism
  • Sexism
  • Sexual Harassment
  • Abuse: Verbal or Physical
  • Intolerance of a person’s religion, gender identity or sexual orientation
  • Behaving in a way that disturbs the enjoyment of the event/discussion for other people
  • Any other actions deemed to be intentionally hurtful, harmful, threatening, or inappropriate

We aim to enforce the right for all members to enjoy their club in an environment that is safe and without risks to health, violence, and aggression.



TCA hosts many types of events, all of which are ‘Friendly’ and will not include any inappropriate or harmful content.


Therefore we will maintain a zero-tolerance towards offensive or obscene language or actions at events that are open to members of all ages.


Members using excessive use of offensive or obscene language at events, to the point where it disturbs other attendees, will be given ONE warning. If the behaviour persists, the member will be required to leave the event.



All TCA members must also adhere to the code of conduct of the VENUE of the event, and venue security and staff must be treated with respect.


Any violation of the Code of Conduct, or any action that a TCA coordinator finds to be inappropriate or disruptive, will result in the offending member being removed from the event. Multiple offenses will result in a termination of the membership,and a life-ban from TCA events. Severe breaches of the Code of Conduct will result in an immediate termination of membership.



Please note, having your membership terminated, or canceling it yourself, will disqualify you from any long-running draws you may have entered.


TCA reserves the right to revoke the membership of any member, at any time.


TCA reserves the right to modify the Disclaimer at any time without notice.
